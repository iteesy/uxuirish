Summary

Throughout my work experience, I've dealt with researching, building, and re-building complex, enterprise dashboard management systems/SaaS for internal use and customers with various admin, managerial, and IC-level roles/permissions.
‍
Examples include but are not limited to:

  • Dashboard for VMware's Audiovisual team to track and monitor the health of conference rooms' devices and software at all office locations worldwide.
  • Dashboard for VMware's Physical Security team to track, monitor, report, and resolve incidents that happen in the vicinity of AMER and APJ campuses. Managers can gather analytic reports of incidents, also assign shifts and deploy work schedules per location.
  • Switching from on-prem to cloud-based solution for To Your Rescue, a non-profit. Our team built a complete redesign of their software so animal shelters and local rescues can centralize their animal inventory from rescue to adoption.
  • At UNIS Fulfillment & Transportation, there was a plethora of dashboards to tackle as that truly represents the logistics & supply chain industry in general. For every phase of the lifecycle from drayage, freight, warehousing, and more thatlead to a vendor, there existed its own complex application!
