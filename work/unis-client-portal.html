Unis Client Portal

Getting an LTL Quote

Project Details

Type: Customer Dashboard, Enterprise
Company: UNIS
Platform: Web, Desktop Client
Timeline: Dec 2018 - March 2019
Tools: Figma, Jira, Trello

Summary

UNIS Client Portal was a product for vendors to manage their shipments with UNIS from their own systems. During the time there wasn't really any innovative tech in the logistics industry besides ShipBob, design systems we're just being talked about, and lastly at this company, design didn't really have a seat at the table.

If you can recall those big rig trucks on freeways Less Than Truckload (LTL) is when your shipment only takes up a small amount of space in the truck and it could hare a space with someone else's inventory order so much like Uber rideshare when that was a thing it takes longer for your items to ship because there's multiple stops that truck is making but because it is less space that it's taking up it's a lot cheaper.

The vice versa it's called FTL which is full truckload where your shipment takes up the entire truck and it goes straight to the destination so it is faster but it's more expensive and because it's more expensive UNIS prioritized this in their business model for faster profit.

During this time, there was already an MVP shipped for LTL customers called Client Portal - TMS where users can view their order history, track their current orders, and manage their account and billing processes.

‍The Opportunity wasn't only about prioritizing LTL but simplifying the traditional getting a quote experience onto our digital product. By being part of this industry, a lot of their processes were very archaic looking and feeling like the fax machine was actually used at the time...and it was.

🚩 Problem

The problem was that there was time-consuming customer delay in relaying the LTL Quote Request.

UNIS was in the early stages of turning newly LTL customers into a long-term relationship, but the initial experience was detracting customers from the friction of lack of timely support from our Customer Service team and Sales.
